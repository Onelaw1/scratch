Step 01

Job Analysis & Decomposition

Moving beyond vague job descriptions. We decompose every role into Atomic Tasks (Action Verb + Object) to enable precise AI suitability analysis.

Key Features

  • • Smart Survey Grid: Excel-like interface for rapid data entry.
  • • Auto-Recommendation: AI suggests tasks based on job title.
  • • Standard Time Library: Benchmarked time data for 500+ tasks.

Expected Effects

  • • 100% Data Granularity: From "HR Manager" to "Drafting Contracts".
  • • Standardization: Unified task taxonomy across the organization.
  • • Foundation for AI: Clean data ready for impact analysis.
Step 02

AI Impact Assessment

Applying the 3-Lens Framework to determine how AI interacts with each task: Substitution, Augmentation, or Generation.

Key Features

  • • Substitution Score: % of task fully automated.
  • • Augmentation Score: % of efficiency gain with human-in-loop.
  • • Generation Factor: New workload created (e.g., verification).

Expected Effects

  • • Nuanced Analysis: Avoids the "AI = Job Loss" fallacy.
  • • Defensible Logic: Scientifically derived impact scores.
  • • Opportunity Spotting: Identifies high-value augmentation targets.
Step 03

Workload Simulation (AI-ADFM)

Calculating the AI-Adjusted Dynamic FTE. We simulate future workload scenarios to determine the scientifically appropriate headcount.

$$ FTE_{target} = \sum (V \times ST \times (1 - Impact)) + FTE_{new} $$
Proprietary Algorithm verified by simulation engine.

Key Features

  • • Scenario Planning: Conservative vs. Aggressive AI adoption.
  • • Dynamic FTE: Real-time recalculation based on parameters.
  • • Gap Analysis: Current FTE vs. Target FTE.

Expected Effects

  • • Audit Defense: Data-backed justification for staffing levels.
  • • Cost Optimization: Identify over/under-staffed areas.
  • • Future-Proofing: Preparedness for 2026-2030 workforce trends.
Step 04

Organizational Re-Design

Transforming "Efficiency Gains" into "Service Value". We reallocate saved hours to high-touch citizen services.

Key Features

  • • Cascading Goals: Aligning team missions with AI strategy.
  • • Role Redefinition: Creating "AI-Augmented" job descriptions.
  • • Agile Structure: Flexible teams for rapid AI deployment.

Expected Effects

  • • Service Quality: More time for complex citizen inquiries.
  • • Innovation: Dedicated teams for AI governance.
  • • Employee Satisfaction: Removal of repetitive drudgery.
Step 05

HR Development & Reskilling

Closing the gap between current skills and future needs. A roadmap for turning bureaucrats into AI-enabled public servants.

Key Features

  • • Skill Gap Analysis: Identifying missing AI competencies.
  • • Training Roadmap: Role-specific AI curriculum.
  • • Change Management: Cultural alignment programs.

Expected Effects

  • • Workforce Readiness: Employees ready for Day 1 of AI adoption.
  • • Retention: Career growth opportunities through upskilling.
  • • Sustainable Growth: Long-term organizational resilience.
